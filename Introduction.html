Introduction:

The Vision and Graph Group (VGG) is a collaborative research initiative jointly established by Nanjing University of Science & Technology (NJUST) and Beijing Normal University (BNU), which historically inherits from the original VGG. VGG's research covers the fields of Computer Vision (CV) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), with a specific focus on graph learning, distribution learning, and visual computing.
The group also explores practical applications such as object detection/segmentation, anomaly detection, and intelligent agents. Its primary aim is to develop novel intelligent theories and methods to address key challenges in CV and AI.
The group currently comprises dozens of members, including three full-time faculty advisors. For more information, please visit our internal website.
